Lease Agreement

This Lease Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into by and between Greenfield Realty (“Landlord”) and Sam Lee (“Tenant”) as of February 15, 2026.

1. Premises. Landlord leases to Tenant the premises located at 123 Main Street, Cityville.
2. Rent. Tenant will pay monthly rent of $1,800 due on the first day of each month.
3. Security Deposit. Tenant shall pay a security deposit of $1,800 at signing. The deposit will be returned at the end of the lease subject to lawful deductions.
4. Repairs. Tenant is responsible for minor repairs and maintenance, while Landlord is responsible for major repairs.
5. Default. If Tenant fails to pay rent within five (5) days after written notice, Landlord may terminate this lease.
6. Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

Signed on the date first written above.
